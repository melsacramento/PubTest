Three things we won't budge on.

Bought from people, not pallets

We name every grower on the bench because we've stood in their orchards. No anonymous boxes off a truck.

Sold within days of picking

Most fruit lands here the morning after it's pulled. If it's been sitting, the price drops — we'd rather you ate it.

Odd shapes welcome

A lumpy pear tastes like a pear. We sell the wonky ones at a discount instead of throwing good fruit away.

A box of "whatever's good"

Tell us how many you're feeding and we'll fill a box each week with the pick of the bench. Leave a note if there's anything you can't stand — we'll swap it.
